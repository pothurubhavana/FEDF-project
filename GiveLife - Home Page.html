About Give a Life

Give a Life is India's National Organ & Tissue Transplant Organisation (NOTTO) digital portal, operating under the Directorate General of Health Services, Ministry of Health & Family Welfare, Government of India.

We facilitate organ donation pledges, deceased donor coordination, and transplant registry management across all states and union territories under the Transplantation of Human Organs and Tissues Act (THOTA), 1994.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between organ donors and recipients, reduce waitlist mortality, and build a culture of voluntary organ donation across India.

Hospital Registration

Hospitals and transplant centres must be registered with NOTTO to perform organ transplants under THOTA 1994.

Required for Registration:

  • ✓ Valid hospital licence from State Medical Council
  • ✓ Registered transplant team (surgeons & coordinators)
  • ✓ NABH / JCI accreditation preferred
  • ✓ State government authorisation letter

To begin hospital registration, contact NOTTO directly:

📞 1800-11-4770  |  ✉ notto@mohfw.gov.in

Recipient Waitlist

The NOTTO waitlist manages patients awaiting organ transplants across India. Currently over 5 lakh patients are waiting for a life-saving organ.

To Register as a Recipient:

  • 1. Contact a registered transplant hospital in your state
  • 2. Get a referral letter from your treating physician
  • 3. Hospital submits your details to ROTTO/SOTTO
  • 4. You are added to the State & National waitlist

For assistance: 1800-11-4770 (Toll Free)

🚨

Report Brain Death

This is for hospital staff and medical professionals only.

If a patient has been declared brain dead by a Medical Board, the hospital is required by law (THOTA 1994) to notify the regional organ sharing network immediately.

Immediate Steps:

1. Notify your State SOTTO immediately
2. Call NOTTO Helpline: 1800-11-4770
3. Maintain donor on ventilator support

Brain death certification requires certification by a 4-member Medical Board including a neurologist.
